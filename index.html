Exploring Biodiversity in U.S. National Parks

Data Visualization & Information Aesthetics | Final Project | Olivia Kasmin

This project visualizes animal and plant species data from 56 U.S. National Parks found in this dataset, which includes data sourced from the National Park Service.

Mapping Park Size & Biodiversity

Some of the smallest parks are home to the highest number of species, such as Redwood National Park and Shenandoah National Park.

Consider Native Species and Biodiversity

The parks with the largest number of species have the lowest percent of native species.

Explore

Compare the categorical distribution of all species, endangered species, and plant and animal species across the national parks.
